Privacy Policy — TinyKittens HQ Timeline Markers

Last updated: 2026/07/23

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or video/chat content, on this device or anywhere else.

What the extension does

While you are watching a TinyKittens HQ live stream on YouTube, this extension reads the public chat messages already visible to every viewer, looking for timestamps that moderators post to mark events (feedings, visits, playtime, and similar). It uses those timestamps to draw clickable markers on the video's own progress bar, so you can jump to a specific moment without scrolling back through chat.

All of this happens locally, inside your own browser. Chat messages are read directly from YouTube's own page data and network responses; nothing is sent to any server operated by us or by any third party.

What the extension stores

The extension uses Chrome's built-in storage APIs to remember your settings (such as text size, and whether labels or hourly markers are shown) and whether you've turned the overlay on or off for a specific video. This information:

  • Stays on your device (or syncs across your own signed-in Chrome browsers, using Google's own Chrome Sync — the same mechanism Chrome uses for your bookmarks and other settings)
  • Is never transmitted to us or to any third party
  • Contains no personal information, browsing history, or video/chat content

Permissions

  • storage — used only to save your settings, as described above.
  • Access to youtube.com — used only to read the page's own live chat data and draw markers on the video player. The extension does not modify, collect, or transmit any other content from the page.

Third parties

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party services of any kind.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same link, with the "Last updated" date above reflecting the change.
